which-key.nvim

Keybinding Discovery

  1. Press , (leader)
  2. Wait ~500ms
  3. Popup shows all mappings with descriptions
This is your cheat sheet!
Use it constantly when learning - no need to memorize everything.
which-key is the most important plugin for learning this config. It groups related commands together (file, git, coc, etc). The popup disappears when you press a key or escape.
